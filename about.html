About Us


Owner and founder Mario Thomas started his truck driving career in 2004. Though he worked with several companies, he realized a commonality between them all: he and his coworkers were repeatedly met with unfair treatment and less than ideal working conditions. Then he got to thinking, why not do it himself?


P Valley Trucking LLC was born in 2012 out of Memphis, TN. Despite moving to Michigan in 2013, threads of Memphis have stayed with P Valley. Its namesake came from the housing project Mario lived in for twenty-five years, and his address there, 3615 Roman Court, went on to inspire his truck number: 3615RC.


P Valley is family-focused. Mario met his wife, Kelly, in 1996 and they have been inseparable ever since. They have five children and know how precious time spent together is. P Valley Trucking LLC strives to make a difference in how drivers are treated in the industry.
